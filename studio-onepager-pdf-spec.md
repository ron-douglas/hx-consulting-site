# Studio One-Pager PDF Spec

## Document Goal
Create a single-page PDF handout for writers’ rooms, producers, and development teams. It should be readable on phone and printable on Letter size.

## File Target
- Final filename: `HX_Consulting_OnePager.pdf`
- Working source: Markdown or HTML (recommended), then export to PDF

## Page Setup
- Size: US Letter (8.5 x 11 in)
- Margins: 0.75 in
- Columns: single column
- Typography: clean sans serif (10.5-12pt body)
- Visual style: minimal, high-contrast, no photos required

## Content Structure (Top to Bottom)

## 1) Header
- Title: `HX Consulting`
- Subtitle: `Psychological realism for film & TV`

## 2) What We Do
Copy:
`We advise writers and creative teams on character psychology, trauma realism, and authentic portrayal of under-resourced communities. Our goal is to help stories feel emotionally credible while avoiding common mental-health tropes and inaccuracies.`

## 3) Services
- `Script & character notes`
- `Writers’ room consulting`
- `On-call advisory during production`

## 4) Who We Are
- `Melanie Hsu, PhD`  
  `Clinical psychologist with specialization in trauma and care for under-resourced populations.`
- `Karen Xie, LMFT`  
  `Licensed therapist with experience supporting individuals, couples, and families navigating trauma and adversity.`

## 5) How Engagement Works
Inline flow:
`Confidential inquiry -> Scoped consult -> Fixed-scope engagement`

Supporting line:
`Available for one-off projects or short-term retainers.`

## 6) Contact
- `contact@hxconsulting.com`

## 7) Required Disclaimer
`Creative consulting only. Not providing therapy or clinical services.`

## Visual Guidance
- Keep whitespace generous
- Use one accent color max
- Avoid dense paragraph blocks
- Ensure black-and-white print legibility

## Accessibility / Readability
- Body copy minimum 10.5pt
- Headings clearly differentiated
- No text over textured backgrounds

## Export QA Checklist
- One page only
- No clipped lines
- Links remain clickable in PDF
- Prints cleanly in grayscale
